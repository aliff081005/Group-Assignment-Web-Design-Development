TYPES OF DRUGS AND THEIR EFFECTS

Learn about common types of drugs and understand their harmful effects on physical and mental health.

Common Types of Drugs

1. Methamphetamine (Syabu)

  • Causes severe addiction.
  • Damages brain cells and memory.
  • Leads to insomnia and paranoia.
  • Increases heart rate and blood pressure.
  • May cause hallucinations.

2. Heroin

  • Highly addictive opioid drug.
  • Causes breathing difficulties.
  • Weakens the immune system.
  • Increases overdose risk.
  • Can lead to coma or death.

3. Cannabis (Ganja)

  • Impairs memory and concentration.
  • Reduces motivation.
  • Causes anxiety in some users.
  • Affects learning ability.
  • May lead to dependency.

4. Cocaine

  • Raises blood pressure.
  • Causes heart attack risk.
  • Leads to anxiety and panic attacks.
  • Highly addictive.
  • Damages nasal tissues.

5. Ecstasy (MDMA)

  • Causes dehydration.
  • Increases body temperature.
  • Affects memory and mood.
  • Can damage brain cells.
  • Leads to depression after use.

6. Ketamine

  • Causes hallucinations.
  • Impairs coordination.
  • Affects memory and thinking.
  • Can damage the bladder.
  • Leads to dependency.

7. LSD

  • Causes visual hallucinations.
  • Distorts reality.
  • Triggers panic attacks.
  • Affects judgement.
  • May cause long-term psychological effects.

8. Morphine

  • Slows breathing.
  • Causes drowsiness.
  • Highly addictive if misused.
  • Leads to dependence.
  • Increases overdose risk.

9. Inhalants

  • Damages the brain.
  • Causes dizziness and confusion.
  • Affects heart function.
  • Leads to memory loss.
  • Can cause sudden death.

10. Fentanyl

  • Extremely powerful opioid.
  • High risk of overdose.
  • Causes breathing failure.
  • Leads to rapid addiction.
  • Can be fatal even in small amounts.
