CAMPAIGN ACTIVITIES

Explore various activities conducted to raise awareness, educate communities, and promote a drug-free lifestyle.

Activities Conducted During The Campaign

Awareness Seminar

Educational seminars are organised to provide information about the dangers of drug abuse, addiction, and prevention strategies.

Poster Design Competition

Students are encouraged to create creative posters that spread anti-drug messages and promote healthy lifestyles.

Educational Workshop

Interactive workshops help participants understand the causes, effects, and prevention methods related to drug abuse.

Community Outreach Program

Volunteers distribute brochures and conduct awareness sessions to educate local communities about drug-related issues.

Sports And Recreation Activities

Sports activities encourage youths to stay active, healthy, and away from negative influences.

Anti-Drug Exhibition

Exhibitions showcase educational materials, real-life stories, and information about the harmful effects of drugs.

Why These Activities Matter

Campaign activities play an important role in increasing awareness, encouraging community participation, and educating people about the dangers of drug abuse. Through active involvement, individuals can gain valuable knowledge and become advocates for a healthier, safer, and drug-free society.
